▼Glossary column definitions
Term Formula Meaning
Net Exposureworst-case loss after offsetsAccounts for offsetting positions (e.g., Over/Under can't both hit)
Weighted RiskΣ payout × P(other legs hit)Expected payout if this selection hits, weighted by the probability each parlay's other legs also hit. Smaller than Max Payout.
Max PayoutΣ full payoutWorst-case maximum liability if every parlay hits in full. Equals the sum of My Risk values in the detail drop-down.
Remainingmax_per_team − net exposureCapacity before risk gate blocks new quotes
Stakes Heldsum of bettor stakes on a gameMoney in pocket; kept if parlays lose
Net Exposure ★max over sides of (payouts owed − opposite stakes)True worst-case loss after offsets. The one to watch.
% of Limitnet / (bankroll × max_per_game_pct)Game’s share of the per-game cap
