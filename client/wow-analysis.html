Takeaways

  1. Two opposite stories across the same week. Weekday window: bigger volume, thinner margin (Period A). Weekend window: bigger volume, slightly fatter margin (Period B). The dividing line falls right at Friday noon — the cutoff between Period A's end and Period B's start.
  2. Theo EV / stake more than doubled between the two current windows (0.62% → 1.04%). Bookend that against the prior week's near-1.00% in both periods, and the message is clear: pricing has become measurably tighter since Friday.
  3. Period A's edge regression is the only soft signal. 5.37% → 3.80% is a real decline week-over-week. The window includes Mon–Wed when many variable changes were in flight. The Period B recovery confirms the changes weren't broken — just transitional.
  4. Volume growth holds across both periods. +46% Period A stake, +47% Period B stake. Bettor demand for Parlay SP's prices is structurally up regardless of the small edge swings — exactly what you'd want to see in a maturing book.
  5. Per-fill EV is down WoW in both periods ($1.78 → $1.48, $2.78 → $2.43). Bettor mix shifted toward smaller individual parlays with about the same total stake — more, smaller tickets. Total EV is the durable metric; per-fill EV is the noise.
